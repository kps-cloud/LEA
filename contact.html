Who We Are

Legacy Executive Aviation is strategically positioned to transform aviation across the SADC region. We exist to close the aviation accessibility gap in Southern Africa by delivering safe, efficient, and premium air mobility solutions.

Our Strength

With immediate access to more than 100 aircraft and a leadership team composed of aviation professionals with over 30 years of experience — including pilots exceeding 15,000 flight hours — our operational capacity and technical expertise set us apart in the regional market.

Regional Impact

We are committed to raising the standard of aviation in Southern Africa through scalable services, dependable flight operations, and a customer-first experience designed for business and executive travelers.
