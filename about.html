About Legacy Executive Aviation

Transforming aviation accessibility across Southern Africa

Legacy Executive Aviation (LEA) is strategically positioned to transform aviation across the SADC region. We are committed to closing the aviation accessibility gap in Southern Africa by delivering safe, efficient, and premium air mobility solutions across private, executive, and commercial aviation sectors.

With immediate access to a fleet of over 100 aircraft and a leadership team bringing more than 30 years of aviation experience—including pilots with over 15,000 flight hours—LEA combines operational strength with deep technical expertise.

Mission

To deliver reliable, world-class aviation services that meet the evolving mobility needs of businesses, governments, investors, and communities across the SADC region.

Vision

To become the benchmark aviation solutions provider in Southern Africa, recognized for safety, operational excellence, and seamless connectivity.
